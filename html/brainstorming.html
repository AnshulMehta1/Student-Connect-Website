Motivation for our Project

Student life drastically progresses and flourishes into certain phases from the start of their academic journey after higher secondary education. This transition from a contemporary educational establishment might be rough for students who want to excel in the preliminary years of university.

Students find themselves in a dilemma for selection of resources in terms of recommended study material to establish a better understanding in their subject of interests apart from the university curriculum, exposure to experienced and veteran interdisciplinary students for proper guidance, and more importantly optimum supervision for their progressive stimuli for future endeavors.

Our Solution

We are making the app Student Connect which helps the user to join an online student community of their college so that it becomes easier for the user to communicate with its peers.

Why Student Connect?

There are several apps available already in the market which provide efficient communication ability for students but they can also initiate a certain level of distraction which our app might not.

What is Student Connect?

We have made the app Student Connect which works on the basis of email instead of phone numbers. To be more specific, it works on the basis of University Email. Every student has a university email though which he/she can receive important updates about the college.

Creating Rooms

Now, here we can argue that there are hundreds of students in the college so, how can a fresher know whom to mail and be sure it’s the correct person he/she is mailing. This is where our app’s main functioning comes in. We have created rooms for different fields of people based on the survey we did for learning about their interests.

For example, we have created groups like Web Development, Android Development, Machine Learning, etc. which helps a student find his/her respective field of interest and can connect with the peers in that group.

What about Hackers/Intruders?

We can also assure that no intruders will be found in the chatrooms which happens nowadays very frequently. The reason is that we will only allow the university students based on their email ids.
